Fatigue Prediction

Long term fatigue may develop into chronic syndrome and eventually harm a person's physical and mental health. Some system needs to be designed to assess tiredness of a person to avoid short term fatigue to be developed into chronic fatigue. Many system exists to detect short term fatigue in a person but most use only eye aperture as a criteria to assess levels of fatigue which might be good for detecting driver drowsiness but, performs poorly in detecting long term fatigue.
